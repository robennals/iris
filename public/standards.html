Community Standards

We want Iris Talk to be a place for civil and socially beneficial discussion between people who trust each other. The following behaviors will not be tolerated:

  • Impersonation: Users or groups pretending to be people or groups that they aren't.
  • Uncivil behavior: Including bullying, harassment, physical threats, or calls for extra-legal violence. It's okay to disagree with people, or to criticise their actions, but you should not do so in a way that implies that other people are unworthy of empathy.
  • Objectionable Content: Including posting content that is offensive, insensitive, upsetting, intended to disgust, or is in expectionally poor taste.
  • Copyright violation: Including links to violating content elsewhere. Other Anti-social behavior: We reserve the right to take action against users for behavior not listed in this document, if we conclude, at our discetion, that it is harmful to the broader Talkful community.

These rules will be stricly enforced and people or groups that violate them may be permenantly banned from Iris Talk.

In addition, group admins may choose to impose stricter rules on the conduct of their group members.

You can report abusive users or groups using the "report abuse" button inside the app. Alteratively, you can email us at abuse@iris-talk.com
